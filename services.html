Recycling & Reuse

Every item we collect is assessed by our team. Furniture in good condition is cleaned, sanitized, and redistributed to families in need or sold at affordable prices. Items beyond reuse are responsibly broken down and sent to certified recycling facilities.

We maintain a zero-landfill policy — your furniture will never simply be thrown away.

Furniture Valuation & Payment

Not sure what your furniture is worth? Our team provides a free on-the-spot valuation. We offer fair market prices for items in good condition, and payment is processed instantly after pickup — no waiting, no confusion.

We accept items even if you don't want payment — you can choose to donate the value to our partner NGOs instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you arrange a pickup?
For our Express plan, we offer same-day or next-day pickups depending on availability in your area. Standard pickups are typically scheduled within 2–3 business days.
How is the furniture valuation done?
Our team assesses the condition, age, material, and market demand of your furniture on the spot. You'll receive a fair, transparent offer before we take anything — there's no obligation to accept.
What if my furniture is in poor condition?
We still collect it! Furniture in poor condition is responsibly broken down and sent to certified recycling facilities. You may not receive payment for heavily damaged items, but disposal is handled free of charge.
Do you disassemble furniture before pickup?
Yes, our team can disassemble large items like wardrobes and bed frames if needed. Just let us know when booking and we'll come prepared.
Which areas in Kolkata do you cover?
We currently serve all major areas of Kolkata including South, North, Central, East, and Salt Lake / New Town. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.
Can I donate my furniture instead of selling it?
Absolutely. Through our Donation Program, you can choose to donate your furniture to verified NGOs and receive a tax-exemption certificate. Simply select the donation option when filling out the form.
