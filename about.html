About Reclaim Furniture

Turning Old Furniture into New Opportunities

Who We Are

Reclaim Furniture is a Kolkata-based platform dedicated to giving used furniture a second life. We connect people who want to responsibly dispose of old furniture with those who need it most — all while keeping thousands of items out of landfills every year.

Whether you're renovating your home, clearing out an office, or simply upgrading your space, we make the process effortless. Schedule a pickup, get a fair price, and know that your furniture is going somewhere it's truly needed.

Our Story

Reclaim Furniture started with a simple realization — perfectly usable furniture was being thrown away, while many people struggled to afford basic necessities.

We built this platform to bridge that gap — connecting those who no longer need furniture with those who truly do.

About the Founder

Created by a passionate developer, this platform reflects a vision of using technology to solve real-world problems.

The goal is simple — reduce waste, help people, and build something meaningful. What started as a small local initiative has grown into a trusted name for responsible furniture disposal in Kolkata.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Every piece of furniture we collect is one less item in a landfill. We believe in a circular economy — where products are reused, repaired, and redistributed rather than discarded.

Our operations are designed to minimize carbon footprint. We optimize our pickup routes, partner with local NGOs for redistribution, and ensure that even items beyond reuse are responsibly broken down for recycling.

♻️ Zero Landfill Goal

We aim to divert 100% of collected furniture from landfills by 2027.

🌍 Carbon Conscious

Optimized pickup routes reduce our transport emissions significantly.

🤝 Community Driven

Partnering with NGOs to redistribute furniture to those in need.

🌿 Our Eco Pledge

We're not just a furniture company — we're a movement. Every decision we make is guided by our responsibility to the planet and to the communities we serve.

  • Ensure every collected item is reused, upcycled, or responsibly recycled
  • Work exclusively with eco-conscious logistics partners
  • Plant a tree for every 10 pickups completed
  • Donate a minimum of 20% of reusable inventory to social welfare organizations
  • Continuously reduce packaging waste in our operations
  • Educate customers on sustainable disposal practices
